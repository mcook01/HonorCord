How This Works

To recognize and honor the great work you are doing in school and in our broader community, we have designed a digital badge system that culminates in earning an Honor Cord worn at your 8th-grade promotion ceremony.

Submit meaningful acts you've completed in school or the community. Each approved act counts as a credit toward your badge. Attach a photo, flyer, letter, or brief description as evidence — your submission will be reviewed by the badge committee.

🔒 You must be signed in with your student Google account to submit this form.
